Bright Cars Story Book

John Love was a recently married young man who had just finished college and landed an entry-level job at a start-up that was aiming to revolutionize the world - “Bright Cars”, a self-driving car company that promised to bring the next generation of automobiles to the industry.

John married a bright and beautiful young woman named Jane, who always saw the best in John even though he was a mediocre man. Love is blind, as they say. Jane thought that John had a hidden interiority, which perhaps was hidden even from himself. When with Jane, John was very energetic and friendly, he would feel motivated to challenge himself, he would even have brighter thoughts. But when he was by himself, he would be just an average guy; maybe below average. He wouldn’t think anything clever, or do anything productive. And so, once the honey-moon effect had left the young couple, Jane found herself married to an uninteresting young man who was not particularly bright or industrious.

Jane grew sad while John grew more distant until one day, Jane decided to bring it all up to John. The couple had a long discussion that profoundly impacted John. John loved Jane with all of his heart, and he knew deep down that she was right. He needed to work on himself, he needed to be more industrious, to develop skills to provide for their blossoming family, he needed to be smart, to become the leader he was always meant to be. He also knew that Jane was feeling unloved and that he had to show her that their old flames were still present in the relationship. He could not let her think that he gave up on her. John focuses on duty

John focused on getting his act together professionally. He knew that Jane would admire him if he became more successful financially. Jane wants a leader and a protector, and the best way to provide for Jane and for the family they were building is for John to focus on developing himself professionally and intellectually. Sure sometimes Jane might feel like he was spending too much time on work, maybe even obsessing about it, but at least she would appreciate his effort and she would know that he was doing it for her and for the family.

John focuses on relationships

John focused on making Jane feel loved everyday. John’s priority was to let Jane know that she is the love of his life, and he wants her to never feel sad again. John will focus on his communication skills, on his enthusiasm, on being more patient, more loving toward his wife and those around him. Sure his work might suffer a little and he may sometimes miss opportunities due to the extra effort he makes in being kind and generous to others, but he knew Jane would appreciate the attention and intention to make her and their family happy.
