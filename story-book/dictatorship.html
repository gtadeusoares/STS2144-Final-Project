Bright Cars Story Book

In this scenario, President Frank McDonnald completely changed the automobile industry in his country, crushing many of the companies that produced non-self-driving cars. There was a lot of political unrest because a huge part of the country felt unheard. However, these people were labelled as crazy people because the policies established by President Frank McDonnald did make the country safer. However, many people lost their jobs since the population was not consulted and had no voice, and many people in the country felt bitterness toward the government for this approach to the issue.
