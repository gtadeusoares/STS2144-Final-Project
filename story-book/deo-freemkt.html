Bright Cars Story Book

Soon after Frank McDonnald came into the presidency, the attention turned toward self-driving cars. Jack Durand was a great entrepreneur who invested all that he had in building the market for self-driving cars. Bright Cars had grown to be the most successful company of autonomous vehicles, and the entire population of the Happy States of Neopolis was debating what should be the role of autonomous vehicles in their society. Some objected to the vehicles due to the high levels of accidents. Others were eager to live in a technologically advanced society where self-driving cars were incorporated into the society. There was a great mix of fear and enthusiasm from the country, and so Frank decided he should partner up with Bright Cars to decide the country’s future regarding autonomous vehicles.

And so, President Frank McDonnald and John Love met to discuss the future of the Happy States of Neopolis. Here is what they decided:

The country should be run like a corporation. Corporations should be free to do whatever is most profitable, because the wealthier the country is, the more money people have to give away. And so all problems end up being balanced out because citizens are willing to pay money to solve society’s issues, and the entrepreneurs will be there to take people’s money and give them what they want. And so, in terms of self-driving vehicles, the Bright Cars should be left alone, with minimal government intervention, to produce their cars. The government invests the money, Bright Cars returns a profit. The market will balance itself out. People will be free to do whatever they want with the self-driving cars.

But how would this be implemented?

Social Media Manipulation

Utilize a social media campaign to influence and manipulate the population. Bribe and finance all the major news media corporations to prevent them from ever red-flagging you, and to have their support in this project, as well as helping to crush anyone speaking out against it and labelling the opposition as extremists.

Social Media Transparency

Utilize a social media campaign with transparency, detailing all the intentions of the projects, and leaving the project open to be criticized and improved by citizens. Allow all sorts of criticism and feedback from the public and media in order to make sure that the project makes everyone happy, even if it takes years to accomplish it.
