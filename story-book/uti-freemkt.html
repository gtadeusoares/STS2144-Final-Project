Bright Cars Story Book

Soon after Frank McDonnald came into the presidency, the attention turned toward self-driving cars. Jack Durand was a great entrepreneur who invested all that he had in building the market for self-driving cars. Bright Cars had grown to be the most successful company of autonomous vehicles, and the entire population of the Happy States of Neopolis was debating what should be the role of autonomous vehicles in their society. Some objected to the vehicles due to the high levels of accidents. Others were eager to live in a technologically advanced society where self-driving cars were incorporated into the society. There was a great mix of fear and enthusiasm from the country, and so Frank decided he should partner up with Bright Cars to decide the countryâ€™s future regarding autonomous vehicles.

And so, President Frank McDonnald and John Love met to discuss the future of the Happy States of Neopolis. Here is what they decided:

The country should be run like a corporation. Bright Cars should be free to do whatever is most profitable for itself and for the government. But they know that self-driving cars are dangerous and unpredictable. Perhaps special roads could be built where only autonomous cars are allowed to drive, to reduce the randomness of events produced by human behavior. John Love and Frank McDonnald know that they need to hear the opinion of the people of the Happy States of Neopolis regarding this issue. Whatever the outcome, at least 51% of the people need to be happy with the decision, and Bright Cars can implement the rest.

But how would this be implemented?

Social Media Manipulation

Utilize a social media campaign to influence and manipulate the population. Bribe and finance all the major news media corporations to prevent them from ever red-flagging you, and to have their support in this project, as well as helping to crush anyone speaking out against it and labelling the opposition as extremists.

Social Media Transparency

Utilize a social media campaign with transparency, detailing all the intentions of the projects, and leaving the project open to be criticized and improved by citizens. Allow all sorts of criticism and feedback from the public and media in order to make sure that the project makes everyone happy, even if it takes years to accomplish it.
