Bright Cars Story Book

President Frank McDonnald and John Love worked together in partnership with the media to push forward their agenda without the concern for the public’s opinion. With the help of Tech Giants and the Mass Media, the two of them were able to revolutionize the country’s automobile industry in record time. There were still, of course, some accidents, but these were swept under the rug. Finally, there was great unrest with the population because many people felt shut down and unheard. Videos began spreading on the internet that exposed the government and industry’s hypocrisy, and many people lost their trust in their country’s institutions, leading to the polarization of political parties.
