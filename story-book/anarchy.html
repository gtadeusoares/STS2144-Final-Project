Bright Cars Story Book

In this scenario, President Frank McDonnald and John Love completely changed the automobile industry in their country. Millions of self-driving cars were produced, with all sorts of different perks, features, and peculiarities. The cars released often malfunctioned, but that is a small price to pay when you are trying to ship your product out as soon as possible. Besides, the customers could always send their faulty car to one of Bright Car’s repair shops. People were mostly left to themselves to use the car however they wanted. This led to a sharp increase in the death rate by accidents with self-driving cars, but that was not really a great concern for the president and Bright Car’s investors and CEO, since everyone in the country benefited financially from the aggressive investment in the country’s automobile industry.
