Bright Cars Story Book

Frank McDonnald had just graduated from Think Well University’s law school, the most prestigious law school of the Happy States of Neopolis. Frank had made many internships in law firms throughout his time as a student. Frank was an incredibly industrious and ambitious man. He was frustrated with how things were in the Happy States of Neopolis and so, growing in his heart each day during his college years, he had the dream of one day becoming the president of Happy States of Neopolis. Frank was a very skillful politician, and so, after much hard work, one day he did become the president of Neopolis. Here is how he did it:

Frank McDonnald and Institutions

Frank McDonnald knew that a country is only as good as its institutions. Every institution should play a part in contributing to society, and so, every institution should somehow be working for or with, and reporting to the government. Frank valued the importance of fair and just laws, a strong police force to be able to enforce those laws, a common educational curriculum so all citizens would share a common basis of knowledge, etc. In his campaign, Frank partnered with multiple institutions, promising them great power if he was elected.

Frank McDonnald and Free Market

Frank McDonnald knew that a country is run by money. Nothing can be done without money. Bad money management puts the country in debt. If the country is in debt, there is no way to innovate, and the country is stuck with paying bills instead of leading the world in innovation and business. Frank didn’t like government controlling institutions, he believed that the market should be free. And so, Frank became very popular with certain companies who funded him in exchange for free regulations once he was elected.
